Level 1 Syllabus

AI Literacy Certificate Curriculum

Course Details

Duration: 8 hours
Format: Self-paced
Level: Entry-level
Prerequisites: None

Learning Outcomes

  • Define generative AI and explain how it works
  • Write effective prompts using basic techniques
  • Identify ethical considerations and bias risks
  • Apply responsible AI practices in workplace tasks

Course Modules

Module 1: AI Fundamentals (2 hrs)

Introduction to generative AI, how models work, and common applications in business

Module 2: Prompt Engineering Basics (2 hrs)

Writing clear prompts, iterating on outputs, and basic prompt patterns

Module 3: Ethics & Responsible Use (2 hrs)

Understanding bias, privacy concerns, and ethical AI implementation

Module 4: Hands-On Practice (2 hrs)

Practical exercises with AI tools and real-world workplace scenarios
