    Framework Domains

    Conceptual Domain

    Understanding AI at a high level

    Beginner

    Can describe AI as "a tool that generates text/images"

    Intermediate

    Understands training data, bias, why outputs vary

    Advanced

    Critically discusses AI's role in society, ethics, and limitations

    Operational Domain

    Using AI tools in practice

    Beginner

    Can try basic prompts, recognizes when output is off

    Intermediate

    Uses AI for work tasks: reports, analysis, content creation

    Advanced

    Designs AI-enhanced workflows, mentors peers in safe usage

    Governance Domain

    Oversight, ethics, and responsible use

    Beginner

    Can name risks: privacy, misinformation, bias

    Intermediate

    Knows organizational policies, applies them correctly

    Advanced

    Leads conversations on AI policy, ethics, and adoption
