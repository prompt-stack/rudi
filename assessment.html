How It Works

1

Pre-Training Baseline

Before training begins, each team member completes the assessment to establish their current AI competency baseline across all 30 skills. This typically takes 20-30 minutes and can be completed remotely.

2

Training Program

Learners progress through their chosen track (Foundation, Business, or Technical), with curriculum specifically designed to build the competencies measured in the assessment.

3

Post-Training Evaluation

After completing the program, learners retake the assessment. Results show growth across all competencies, with visual before/after comparison in their personalized radar chart.

4

Ongoing Measurement

Organizations can re-assess teams quarterly or annually to track long-term AI literacy development and identify emerging training needs as technology evolves.
