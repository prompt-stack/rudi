RUDI & Did You Know Publishing, Inc.

RUDI (Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence) has partnered with Did You Know Publishing, Inc. to provide comprehensive AI training and certification programs. This partnership combines RUDI's expertise in responsible AI education with Did You Know Publishing's training delivery infrastructure.

When you contact Did You Know Publishing, Inc., you're accessing the full RUDI curriculum and certification program. This collaboration ensures that organizations receive the highest quality AI training with seamless enrollment, delivery, and certification processes.

All RUDI training programs are TechCred-eligible
