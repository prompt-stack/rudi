About the founder

BH

Brandon Z. Hoff

Chief AI Facilitator

Brandon often describes himself as "not a computer scientist"—and that's exactly the point. His background is in economics, cooperative business models, and research at the intersection of technology and human systems.

After founding and scaling Heritage Hill Cooperative to ~$3M in revenue, Brandon saw how technology could either extract value or create it. When ChatGPT launched in 2022, he recognized this wasn't just another tool—it was a fundamental shift in how we learn, work, and create.

What started as TikTok tutorials (now 80,000+ followers) evolved into corporate workshops, university courses, and advisory work with legislators and Fortune 500 companies. He founded RUDI because he kept seeing the same pattern: organizations buying AI tools without building the literacy to use them effectively.

Brandon comes from a family of educators, and that shapes everything RUDI does. "Technology is easy," he says. "Adoption is hard. That's what we solve."
