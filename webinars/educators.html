Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend this webinar?

This webinar is designed for K-12 teachers, special education teachers, school administrators, district leaders, instructional coaches, and Educational Service Center staff in Ohio interested in learning about AI tools and TechCred funding opportunities.

Is this webinar really free?

Yes! This is a free educational webinar with no obligation. Our goal is to help Ohio educators understand how to access state-funded AI training through the TechCred program.

Will I receive a recording if I can't attend live?

Yes! All registrants will receive a recording of the webinar and presentation materials within 24 hours of the event, even if you can't attend live.

What is TechCred and how does it work?

TechCred is an Ohio state program that reimburses employers (including schools and districts) for technology-focused employee training. You complete approved training, and the state reimburses up to $2,000 per employee. We'll explain the full application process during the webinar.

Do I need any AI experience to attend?

No prior AI experience is required! This webinar is designed for educators at all levels, from complete beginners to those with some AI familiarity. We'll cover foundational concepts and practical applications.

Can I share this with colleagues?

Absolutely! We encourage you to share this opportunity with other educators at your school or district. The more educators who understand TechCred funding, the better.
