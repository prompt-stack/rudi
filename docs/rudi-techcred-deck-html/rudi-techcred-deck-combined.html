Measurable Competencies

30-Point Competency Matrix: Conceptual Dimension Example

Level
Non-Technical User
Technical User
No Exposure
Has never heard of generative AI
Has no awareness of AI/ML technologies
Pre-Beginner
Has heard the term "AI," maybe ChatGPT, but cannot explain it
Knows AI exists but confuses it with automation; no exposure to models
Beginner
Can describe AI as "a tool that generates text/images"
Knows AI involves training data and models; aware of major providers
Intermediate
Understands basics: training data, bias, why outputs vary
Understands parameters, embeddings, fine-tuning; can explain architectures
Advanced
Can critically discuss AI's role in society, ethics, and limitations
Can explain trade-offs in model design, architectures, and evaluation metrics
